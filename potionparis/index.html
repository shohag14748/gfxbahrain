HOW TO REFILL YOUR ICONIC VIAL

Reinventing the tradition of perfume fountains born in the 18th century. Our 10ml refillable fragrances which slot inside our iconic Vials, took inspiration from the exquisite perfume bottles of historic major perfume houses, often designed by famous Crystal Engravers, which were refilled as desired with their original fragrance.

1. Take your Iconic Vial

2. Select your Potion Fragrance Refill

3. Unscrew studded neck anti-clockwise

4. Insert your Fragrance Refill

5. Screw on studded neck clockwise

6. Spritz in style

CRUELTY-FREE, TOXIN-FREE & SUSTAINABLE

Sourced and composed using rare, precious, ethically farmed and sustainable ingredients from around the world. We are completely cruelty free with no animal testing. By being toxin free we ensure there are no parabens and phthalates which are among the top “dirty ingredients” in the beauty industry in our scents.

RETURN. REFILL. REPEAT

All our fragrances come in handcrafted 10ml refills that fit perfectly into our patented Iconic vials. Return, Refill, Repeat is our sustainable concept that encourages the return of the 10ml refills to be cleaned, sanitised and reused.

A sustainable program that encourages the return of the empty refills by rewarding you with a free fragrance refill (2x10ml) of your choice (when you send back 10 empty refills).
